About Naomi

I love watching dogs learn and gain confidence. I am a dog trainer in-training. Meanwhile, I provide dog walking in the SE15 London postcode. I study and use force-free, positive methods only. I am doing a Diploma in Canine Behaviour; a Diploma in Canine Coaching; and a Certificate course in Reactive Behaviour through Canine Principles. I assist dog trainer Louise Taylor with dog classes and some 1-2-1 training. I am dog first aid certified, have a DBS certificate and volunteer one day at week at Battersea Dog & Cats Home's kennels (no dog walks on Wednesdays!) so I am quite familiar with reactive and rescue dogs.

Services

Dog Walking: I offer walks specific to your dog's needs, which we would discuss in advance. I generally don't do group walks, unless they are dogs from the same household. Every dog is different and I look forward to getting to know your's!
